Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born July 7, 1981, is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicketkeepers and captains in cricket history. He is known for his cool demeanor and powerful hitting style.

Achievements

  • Champions Trophy (2013)
  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna (Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award): 2007
  • Five-time IPL Champion as CSK captain: 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023
